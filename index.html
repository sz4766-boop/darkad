What this site covers

This website introduces the indie game company Ironmace and explains, at a high level, how anti‑cheat teams reason about adversarial players. I previously coordinated with an anti‑cheat group to help fix exploit‑adjacent defects and reduce abuse. The goal here is educational: understand trust boundaries, threat modeling, and practical mitigations—without sharing cheat-building instructions.
